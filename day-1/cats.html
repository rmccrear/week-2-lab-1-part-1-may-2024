Dogs

Who doesn't like dogs? They are super cuddly and and great protectors. Let's look at some reasons why dogs are great!

Why dogs are great!

Dogs are the best pets you could ever have! Don't believe me? Read the next paragraph, and you'll see why!

Dogs are very playful and generally keep you active. They love to take walks outdoors or sit inside and enjoy their favorite snack. If you train them well, they can do things such as open doors, roll over, and play fetch.
